Yoga can be defined as a system of physical and mental practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. The main aim of yoga is to
promote harmony between mind and body.Yoga is based on the principle of ‘unity in diversity’.It helps to develop physical and mental

also help reduce stress and improve your breathing. Yoga is an amazing practice that can benefit your mind, body and soul. If you are
looking for a way to improve your health and wellbeing, then yoga is definitely worth considering. With so many different types of yoga
available,there is sure to be a style that suits you.
