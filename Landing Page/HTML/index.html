About Us

The Wellness Club Gym Xpress is a premium lifestyle health club chain.
We are a corporate & professionally managed gym services group in Krishna nagar,
Janakpuri, Meera Bagh & Vikaspuri . Our mantra Is premium delivery at middle class pricing.
Our founders have years of rich experience in science of exercise, protocol in services and
management and Avid exercisers themselves. The Wellness Club Gyms is a confluence of cardio
workouts & high-performance strength exercises. Depending on the model of the gyms we have,
Our services also include Zumba, Salsa, Yoga, Bhangra, Kick Boxing, Crossfit and Functional
Training.These workouts are available in full/ part at various centers we have.Steam,
Showers, Change Room are also part of our few of our facilities.
